Jon Snow - the homie

Jon Snow is a G. He is the bastard son of Ned Stark, the Warden of the North, and noble leader of House Stark. His birth mother is a mystery, but he was raised by Catlyn Stark, who could never bring herself to love him, and would often blame things on him. Despite a tough upbringing, Jon has proven to be an intelligent, brave, noble, and skilled warrior. He rose to the rank of Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, before being stabbed to death by his own men, who disagreed with his strategy.
